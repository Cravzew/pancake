Pancake

Tea Break Pancake is a food item that the player can cook. The recipe for Tea Break Pancake is obtainable from Good Hunter for 2,500 Mora after reaching Adventure Rank 15

Depending on the quality, Tea Break Pancake revives and restores 250/400/550 HP to the target fallen character with a 120-second cooldown. Like most foods, this cannot target other players' characters in Co-Op Mode.

How to obitain

  • Recipe: Sold by Good Hunter
